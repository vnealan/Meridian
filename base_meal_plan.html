Daily Health & Nutrition Plan

Breakfast (8:00 AM)
• Scrambled eggs (2 eggs) with spinach and tomatoes
• Whole grain toast (1 slice) with avocado
• Greek yogurt (1 cup) with berries
Supplements:
• Vitamin D3 (4000 IU) - Take with breakfast for better absorption
• Iron Bisglycinate (25 mg) - Best taken on an empty stomach
Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)
• Handful of mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, and cashews)
• Sliced apple
Supplements:
• Vitamin B12 (1000 mcg)
Lunch (12:30 PM)
• Grilled chicken breast (4 oz) with quinoa (1 cup) and steamed broccoli
• Olive oil and lemon dressing
Supplements:
• Magnesium Glycinate (400 mg)
• Duloxetine (60 mg) - Take with food
Afternoon Snack (3:00 PM)
• Hummus (1/4 cup) with carrot and cucumber sticks
• Cottage cheese (1/2 cup)
Supplements:
• Amitriptyline (10 mg)
Dinner (6:30 PM)
• Baked salmon (4 oz) with sweet potato (1 medium) and asparagus
• Side salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and vinaigrette
Supplements:
• Ibuprofen (400 mg) - If needed for pain relief
Evening Snack (8:30 PM)
• Oatmeal (1/2 cup dry) made with water or almond milk
• Topped with banana and cinnamon
Supplements:
• Magnesium Glycinate (if not taken earlier)

Important Notes:

• Iron Bisglycinate and Magnesium Glycinate should be spaced at least 2 hours apart
• Monitor for any symptoms of gastrointestinal discomfort, especially when combining Duloxetine and Ibuprofen
• Ensure proper hydration throughout the day
• Consult with a healthcare provider if unusual symptoms occur
